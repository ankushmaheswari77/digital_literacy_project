# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s

- Think before posting – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Maintain privacy settings – Keep your personal information secure.
- Use professional language – Especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
- Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading fake news.
- Be respectful in comments and messages – Maintain a positive digital presence.

## ❌ Don’ts

- Don’t share personal details – Avoid posting phone number, address, OTP, etc.
- Don’t post offensive or abusive content – It can harm your reputation.
- Don’t click on suspicious links – They may lead to scams or hacking.
- Don’t overshare daily activities – It can compromise your safety.
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It reflects poorly on you.
